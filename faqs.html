We're here to help

We've gathered some of our most common questions below, but you can contact us directly for any information or interest. Customer Support is also available for you 24/7. Call us at 021 38654444 or you can email us at cs@bykea.com

Customers

I got into an accident, now what?

We're here to support you. After ensuring you're safe, please contact our 24/7 Helpline immediately at 021 38654444 to report the Incident.

Can I use BYKEA without a smartphone?

Yes, you can call our booking center to request services if you don't have access to the app.

How can I start a private for-hire?

You can book a ride through the Bykea app by selecting the 'Ride' service and entering your destination.

How do I activate Bykea Account?

Simply download the app and sign up with your mobile number. You'll receive an OTP to activate your account.

Partners

How do I become a Bykea Partner?

Applicants are Entrepreneurs looking to grow their business with Bykea. Please visit our nearest Bykea Franchise center for more details relative to becoming a Partner.

I want to become a Bykea Partner but don't own a bike

We have various partner programs. Please contact our partner support team for information on bike leasing or rental options.

Businesses

My parcel got damaged during delivery, now what?

In the rare event of parcel damage, please report it immediately through the app or call our business support line. Our team will investigate and process claims according to our policy.

How can I start a Bykea Franchise?

Details for franchising are available on our 'Franchise' page. You can also submit an inquiry form there.
